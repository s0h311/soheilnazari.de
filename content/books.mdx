# Book Recommendations

---

### For Everbody

- James Clear's _Atomic Habits_ --- to achieve anything significant, you'll need strong fundamentals.
  This book provides you with that and more.

### For Students (German)

- Daniel Hunold's _Motiviert Studiert_ --- a good companion for any student who struggles getting the _Stoff_
  inside of his head

### For Those Who Seek More

- Yuval Noah Harari's _Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind_ --- why is our society the way it is? Why is
  enough never really enough? Why do you exist?
